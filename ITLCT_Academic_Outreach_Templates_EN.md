## ⚠️ Email Etiquette Guidelines

### Do's:
1. ✅ Use formal address (Dear Professor [Last Name])
2. ✅ Reference their specific work
3. ✅ Keep concise (<500 words)
4. ✅ Provide clear exit ("We understand if too busy")
5. ✅ Emphasize open science (MIT license)
6. ✅ Wait 30 seconds between emails

### Don'ts:
1. ❌ Don't ask for endorsement
2. ❌ Don't send bulk emails (use BCC if necessary)
3. ❌ Don't follow up more than twice
4. ❌ Don't send attachments >5MB
5. ❌ Don't use informal language
