## 1. Reddit Posting Plan

### r/physics

**Title**: "ITLCT v20.0: A unified framework for time, life, consciousness, and quantum gravity — 47 axioms, 200 theorems, 80 equations with 23 falsifiable predictions"

**Content Strategy**:
- Lead with the experimental predictions (physicists value falsifiability above all)
- Emphasize Tier 1 predictions testable with current technology
- Acknowledge the speculative nature of Tier 3 predictions openly
- Link to the experimental roadmap post
- Flair: Theoretical Physics or General Physics

**Tone**: Humble, evidence-focused. Avoid grand claims; let the framework speak for itself.

**Expected Reception**: Mixed. Physics subreddit is skeptical of "theories of everything." Counter by emphasizing specific, quantitative, falsifiable predictions. Be prepared for tough questions about mathematical rigor.

## 3. arXiv Discussion Group Strategy

### Target Categories

- **quant-ph**: Quantum consciousness, information-theoretic foundations
- **gr-qc**: Consciousness-gravity coupling predictions
- **q-bio.NC**: Neural information theory, consciousness measures
- **physics.gen-ph**: General theoretical frameworks (use cautiously — this category is sometimes viewed skeptically)

### Approach

**Phase 1 — Preparation (Week 1)**:
- Compile a formal arXiv-style preprint from ITLCT v20.0 materials
- Format with proper LaTeX, equations, and references
- Have at least 2 external reviewers check the manuscript
- Identify the best primary category (likely quant-ph or q-bio.NC)

**Phase 2 — Submission (Week 2)**:
- Submit to arXiv (may require endorsement for some categories)
- Cross-list to relevant secondary categories
- Announce on social media and ResearchGate

**Phase 3 — Engagement (Weeks 3–4)**:
- Monitor comments and questions
- Respond to all substantive critiques within 48 hours
- Update the preprint based on valid feedback
- Cite and engage with related work identified through arXiv discussions

### arXiv Preprint Structure (Proposed)

1. **Title**: "ITLCT v20.0: A Unified Information-Theoretic Framework for Time, Life, Consciousness, and Quantum Gravity"
2. **Abstract**: 150 words, emphasizing falsifiable predictions
3. **Introduction**: Fragmentation problem, motivation
4. **Framework**: Axioms, theorems, equations (summarized)
5. **Predictions**: All 23 experimental predictions with protocols
6. **Discussion**: Comparison with IIT, free energy principle, entropic gravity
7. **Conclusion**: Call for experimental collaboration
8. **Appendices**: Full axiom list, key proofs
