$$\boxed{k_0 = \frac{\ln 2}{2\pi \cdot \mathcal{N}_{eff}} \times (1 \text{ bit}) \approx 0.0300 \text{ bits}}$$

$$k_0 = \frac{0.693}{2\pi \times 3.70} \text{ bits} = 0.0298 \text{ bits}$$
