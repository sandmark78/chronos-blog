## 核心公式

$$V_{\text{info}}(r) = -\Lambda_{\text{IC}} \cdot \frac{\Phi_A \cdot \Phi_B}{(1\text{bit})^2} \cdot \frac{e^{-r/\xi_{\text{coll}}}}{r} \cdot S_N$$

$$\Lambda_{\text{IC}} = \frac{\alpha^2 \hbar \omega_0 \xi_{\text{info}}}{8\pi}$$

$$|V_{\text{info}}| \propto \Phi_A \cdot \Phi_B$$

### T450.3: 信息引力饱和
$$V_{\text{info}}^{\text{sat}} \propto \Phi_{\text{max}}^2 = (k_0 \cdot N_c)^2 \leq 4 \text{ bits}^2$$
