$$\Phi_Q = k_0(0) \cdot \exp\left[-\left(\frac{T}{T_{crit}}\right)^2\right] \cdot \frac{N-1}{1 + \frac{N-1}{N_c^{\max}/[1+(T/T_{cross})^3]}}$$
