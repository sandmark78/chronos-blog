## 在论文中的处理策略

### Section 5.5 (跨主体整合) 引用措辞

**建议原文:**

> "The concept of measuring integrated information across multiple agents was first explored by Engel & Malone (2017/2018), who demonstrated that group-level Φ can exceed the sum of individual Φ values. However, their work provided empirical measurement protocols without an analytical formula. Our T411 (Theorem 411) extends this foundation by deriving the closed-form expression ΔΦ_cross-subject = log₂(1 + R_c × (N_c - 1)), which quantitatively predicts the integration gain as a function of inter-agent coupling strength (R_c) and agent count (N_c). This analytical form enables testable predictions for collective consciousness scenarios, including the scaling behavior with group size and the critical coupling threshold for emergent group-level integration."

**中文翻译:**

> "跨多个主体测量整合信息的概念最初由 Engel & Malone (2017/2018) 探索，他们证明了群体层面的Φ可以超过个体Φ值之和。然而，他们的工作提供了实证测量方案，而非解析公式。我们的 T411 (定理 411) 扩展了这一基础，推导出了封闭形式表达式 ΔΦ_cross-subject = log₂(1 + R_c × (N_c - 1))，该公式定量预测了整合增益作为主体间耦合强度 (R_c) 和主体数量 (N_c) 的函数。这一解析形式使得集体意识场景的可检验预测成为可能，包括随群体规模的缩放行为和涌现群体层面整合的临界耦合阈值。"
