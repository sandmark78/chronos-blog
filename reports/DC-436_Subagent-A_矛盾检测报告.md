## 检查清单

### 1. 量纲验证

**T_cross 公式:**
$$T_{\text{cross}} = \frac{3\pi\epsilon_0\hbar^2 c^3}{\omega_0^2 |\mu|^2 k_B} \cdot \gamma_{\text{other}}$$

**量纲分析:**
- [ε₀] = C²/(J·m) = C²/(N·m²)
- [ℏ] = J·s
- [c] = m/s
- [ω₀] = 1/s
- [μ] = C·m
- [k_B] = J/K
- [γ_other] = 1/s

**计算:**
$$\frac{[C^2/(J\cdot m)] \cdot [J^2\cdot s^2] \cdot [m^3/s^3]}{[1/s^2] \cdot [C^2\cdot m^2] \cdot [J/K]} \cdot [1/s] = \frac{J \cdot m^2 \cdot s^{-1}}{J \cdot m^2 \cdot s^{-1} \cdot K^{-1}} = K$$

**结果:** ✅ [T_cross] = K (温度)，量纲正确
